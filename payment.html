How to Make a Payment

To access premium features or listings, follow these simple steps:

  1. Dial *165# for MTN or *185# for Airtel on your phone.
  2. Select "Send Money".
  3. Enter the following phone number:
    • MTN: 0786422819
    • Airtel: Coming Soon
  4. Enter the amount: UGX 10,000 (or as indicated).
  5. Complete the payment by accepting the transaction on your phone.

After you submit your details, you will be redirected to your phone to confirm and complete the payment.
